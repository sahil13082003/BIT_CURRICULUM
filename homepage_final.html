About Us

The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) is a statutory body in India responsible for regulating and coordinating technical education and management education in the country. Established in 1945 as an advisory body, it was granted statutory status in 1987 through an Act of Parliament. AICTE's primary role revolves around promoting the quality of technical education in India. It formulates policies and norms, approves new programs and institutes, and monitors their functioning to ensure adherence to standards. It's also involved in providing guidelines for the development of technical education, fostering innovation, and encouraging research in technical fields.
       The council's jurisdiction extends to various aspects of technical education, including engineering, pharmacy, architecture, applied arts and crafts, hotel management and catering, management education, and more. It's tasked with maintaining the standards and ensuring the quality of education and infrastructure in these domains. AICTE plays a crucial role in fostering collaborations between industries and technical institutions to enhance the employability of graduates and align educational programs with industry requirements. It also focuses on promoting entrepreneurship among students and faculty members. Additionally, AICTE has been instrumental in promoting and regulating online education, ensuring that it meets quality standards and widens access to technical education across the country. Overall, the AICTE acts as a key regulatory body that shapes and oversees the landscape of technical education in India, aiming to maintain high standards, encourage innovation, and create a skilled workforce to meet the demands of a rapidly evolving global economy.

Our Vission

  • To promote quality in technical education by setting and maintaining high standards for technical institutions and programmes.
  • To plan and coordinate the development of the technical education system throughout the country.
  • To regulate and maintain norms and standards in the technical education system.

Our Mission

  • Developing and implementing policies and regulations for technical education.
  • Providing financial support to technical institutions.
  • Promoting research and development in technical education.
  • Collaborating with industry to make technical education more relevant and responsive to the needs of the industry.

Our Goals

  • Increasing the intake of students into technical education
  • Improving the quality of technical education
  • Developing new technical education programs to meet the needs of industry
  • Promoting innovation and entrepreneurship in technical education
